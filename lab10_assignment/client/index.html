IMDb Top Rated Films

The list of Top 250 films on IMDb is an assortment of popular films which are ranked based on their average rating.

The display of the Top 250 on IMDb as-is is fun to skim through, but not so easy to sort through.

This website aims to make IMDb's information quicker to digest when wanting to quickly pick a new movie to watch.
